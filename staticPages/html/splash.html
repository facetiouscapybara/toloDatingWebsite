About the App

Find people near you. Now.

Tolo is a dating app that pairs people who are within walking distance of each other. You could be going out for a cup of coffee and just want to have a spontaneous converation.

Women get to start things off

Tolo takes the pressure out of dating. Women don't have to worry about guys sending messages when they are just out with the girls. It is up to the Woman to decide if she wants to start a conversation.
